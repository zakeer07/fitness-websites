⚠️ Flare Day Adjustments
On flare days, lower protein slightly and simplify meals:
• Use water instead of milk in shake
• Cooked veg only (skip raw salads)
• Plain rice + chicken + cooked carrots is a safe meal
• Avoid dairy, spice, and high-fibre raw foods
• Note it in the Tracker if you use notes
